NUCLENOIR

Overview
Take the role of a spy infiltrating a nuclear power plant under construction during the cold war.
Pass yourself off as the safety inspector and sabotage their systems in this first-person stealth puzzle game.

Details

  • Development time: 6 weeks
  • Collaborators: 12
  • Platform: PC
  • Engine: Unity

Contribution

  • Puzzle management and progression
  • Character interactions
  • UI management
